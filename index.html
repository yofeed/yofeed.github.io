YOFEED

Get a Yo whenever your favorite feeds update.

How it works

1. Copy a valid RSS (or Atom) feed's URL to your clipboard

2. Add YOFEED to your contacts and send us a Yolink

SENDING LINK
Press and hold YOFEED in the Yo app

3. YOFEED will Yo you with the latest link from your RSS feed every time it updates!


Once you've added at least one feed, send a plain Yo (without link) to YOFEED to manage your subscriptions.
